🔧 Mechanism Design Summary

Mechanism A: Convergence-Aware Attention (CAA)

  • Principle: Delta deep tokens have highest MI with labels
  • Implementation: Learnable token depth weights + convergence score
  • Result: Best performance, stable training

Mechanism B: Stability Attention Bias (SAB)

  • Principle: Offset concentrates 48% attention on Token 0
  • Implementation: Temperature scaling + Token 0 penalty
  • Result: F1 improved (0.42 vs 0.33), but AUC lower

Mechanism C: Dual-Stream Architecture (DSA)

  • Principle: Mixed Offset+Delta achieves best deep attention
  • Implementation: Parallel streams + Cross-attention + Gated fusion
  • Result: Unstable training, needs tuning

For High-Dimensional Datasets (D > 100)

  • Alternative: Standard Delta strategy (simpler, comparable)
  • Not recommended: SAB alone - lower AUC
